Off-chain coordination that complements on-chain governance

Ethereum Classic DAO LLC provides administrative and operational functions where a legal counterparty is required—contracting, grant disbursement interfaces, and partnership engagement. It does not control on-chain protocol upgrades or treasury execution, which remain governed by ECIPs and on-chain contracts.

For Olympia-specific protocol governance and on-chain DAO mechanics, see OlympiaDAO.org and the ECIP repository.

Typical Responsibilities

  • Operational coordination for grants and partnerships that require a legal counterparty.
  • Payment processing and fiat on/off-ramp assistance for funded projects when approved through on-chain governance.
  • Administrative support for community programs, events, and outreach.
  • Transparent reporting and record-keeping for off-chain activities tied to on-chain proposals.
